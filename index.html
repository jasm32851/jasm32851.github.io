Professional Experience

Sharma Research Group

Research Assistant/Doctoral Candidate (Fall 2016 - Present)

Our group is interested in developing fast and accurate methods to compute the electronic structure of highly multireference molecules. I'm currently working on the Semistochastic Heatbath Configuration Interaction (SHCI) project to connect this algorithm with the PySCF Quantum Chemistry package. Our group has implemented the SHCI algorithm in the Dice package and in the future, I hope to use Dice/PySCF to build potential energy surfaces and use these to study the surface hopping dynamics of metal centers found in biological molecules.
