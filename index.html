Computer Science Meets Life Science

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Chemical Physics at the University of Colorado, Boulder. My research focuses on developing and implementing new quantum mechanical methods to calculate the properties of molecules and materials. I'm particularly interested in efficient parallel algorithms that can utilize the power of high performance computing (HPC) resources to tackle pressing problems in the scientific community and packaging in them in software that is easy and straightforward to use.

If you would like to know more about my research you can check out the Code, Publications, or Talks pages. If you are interested in collaborating or you have a cool idea you think I'd be interested in shoot me an email or connect with me using one of the links at the bottom of the page.
